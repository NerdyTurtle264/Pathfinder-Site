Stain: An eccentric man who clashes with everything you would expect or have heard. He easily carries a steel safe on his back whenever he leaves the base.

Desma Fide: Second in command of your outpost. She walks with the measured purposeful gait of a person used to maneuvering with armor and weapons on her hips. She is missing her left arm and has it replaced with a mechanical one built by the twins. She has been relegated to this region due to a previous battle where she went against orders to save her group and was reprimanded harshly. She has an intense hatred of the leader of the battle where this happened. Brigadier General Stain.

Ignacio Ingenium: A tall thin man who is Desma's right hand man. He prefers to dress in well tailored clothes and walks with almost inhuman haste at almost all times. He has been with Desma since before her disgrace and trusts her even more because of it.

"Jynx" Jagna Tenabre: A noble of house Tenabre. She is in charge of the armory at the base. A bit of a shut in, she is rarely seen outside the armory. She is indifferent when it comes to military protocol with regards to hierarchy. Her penchant for both explosives and falling down make her someone many are wary of.

"Hyde" Ignatia Tenabre: “Oldest” of the twins. She is the more level headed of the two often keeping Jynx in check. A bit of a gruff but has her moments when you get to know her.

Fulvia Tana: The stable girl for your barracks. She can often times be found caring for the horses in the day. After dusk when her work is done she can be found sparing with Cadoc. An odd thing to find two helots doing.

Cadoc of Clan RockTusk: The cook for your outpost. His meals aren't the best but they are always hearty and wonderful to come back to after a long mission. During the day he is usually found in the kitchen preparing the next meal. After dinner he has been seen walking into the forest to his hidden garden. After his work is done him and Fulvia can oftentimes be found sparing.

Prologue

Patriam suffered a magic plague 500 years ago where all magic was neutralized. The people in the time since have been developing technological means of accomplishing the same sort of effects however. In a land devoid of magic including healing magic the people have adapted to this wholeheartedly and even have begun to incorporate technology into their own bodies, sometimes as a way of replacing limbs lost in battle, sometimes as a symbol of their class. The people of this land are scared and proud of it. The cause of the magic plague is lessening now though and magic is beginning to have a resurgence. Some are using it for it's original purposes and some are using it to power technological creations never thought possible before. The country has a conscription service for the citizens. Everyone has to spend 10 years in the military once they reach the age of adulthood. After the 10 years people can choose to stay in the service of their country and many do. You will all be playing members of the Patriam military at the start of their career assigned to a smaller unit towards the border of the country.
